About Green Tech 2023

The Camellia Institute of Technology is excited to announce our upcoming science exhibition, focused on the theme of green technology. We invite all students to participate in teams of four members, and showcase hardware or physical projects that aim to make our environment more sustainable.


Some examples of projects that would fit this theme include:



  • A solar-powered water filtration system for rural communities
  • A device for measuring air pollution levels in cities
  • A vertical garden that can be used to grow food in urban areas

    • We encourage students to think creatively and come up with their own unique ideas. The goal of this exhibition is to raise awareness about the importance of green technology and inspire students to take action in their communities. We look forward to seeing the innovative projects that our students will develop. This exhibition will be held on 15th march 2023 10:00 am at our college campus. Let's all come together to make our world a greener and cleaner place.
